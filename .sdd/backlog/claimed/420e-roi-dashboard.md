# 420e — ROI Dashboard: Prove Business Value
**Role:** frontend  **Priority:** 3 (medium)  **Scope:** small

## Problem
Gartner: "40% of agentic AI projects cancelled due to unclear business value."

## Design
Track: time saved (estimated vs actual), cost per task, agent success rate, lines of code generated/reviewed. Show: "This run saved ~2.5 hours of manual work at $0.42 cost." Exportable report for management.
